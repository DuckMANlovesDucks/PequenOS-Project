The Philosophy

Why build another OS?

Modern operating systems have become bloated, shipping with gigabytes of preinstalled software you'll never use. Background telemetry, unnecessary services, and heavy GUIs drain your battery and slow down your machine.

PequeOS was born from a desire to return to the roots of computing. Created by duckmanlovesducks, it's an educational journey turned into a functional, beautiful, and hyper minimalist Linux distribution.
